## Recording mechanics

1. **Nera is fixed** (retired-model config repaired 3 Jul) - do one warm-up Nera exchange off-camera first so the on-camera one is snappy, and pre-choose a question with a strong seeded answer (from the May demo corpus: the "weakest pillar" question produced a good cited answer - verify it still reads well).
2. Use the **seeded demo engagements** (three exist: two strategic-planning, one research-intelligence). Pick the strategic-planning one - its vocabulary (Priorities/Initiatives/Lenses) reads instantly to reviewers.
3. Record at 1080p minimum, browser chrome hidden (F11), cursor deliberate. OBS or Loom; Loom's camera-bubble works well for the 0:00 hook if you'd rather not cut to a separate talking head.
4. **One take per segment, assemble in Descript** - and Descript can strip filler words and tighten gaps to bring it in under 2:00 without rushing your delivery.
5. Upload YouTube **unlisted**. Title: "Carlorbiz Strategic Tool - 2-minute demo (Google for Startups)". Turn OFF ads/end-screens. Test the URL in an incognito window before pasting into the application.
